Triumphant Risings

"I have everything within me that I need in order to live my highest life."

Triumphant Risings: Affirm Your Rising and Wake with the Day is a 40 day affirmation book to help aid you in setting forth your intentions with the rising of each day. As we are constantly creating and co-creating our realities we must be intentional in doing so. Life and death are in the power of the tongue and by speaking into yourself you are unleashing the limitless power to manifest what you speak into your present reality.


Who is Trikeria Johnson?

  • Philanthropist, Author, and Transformational Speaker

    The Creation of Self

    Trikeria Johnson was born in Columbia, SC to a single mother who knew from day one, that she was destined to be triumphant. Throughout her time in high school, and at Howard University , while excelling in school, she was involved in a number of extracurriculars, including being the captain of the track team, as well as a leader of her university, as Miss College of Arts and Sciences.

  • A Natural Leader

    The Sharing of Self

    In these leadership positions, Trikeria has been able to develop a thorough understanding of what it means to be a leader, and how to grow yourself into positions that may be bigger than yourself. She brings all of that wisdom into her first book "Triumphant Risings", a 40 day devotional about becoming the best version of yourself. She believes that people not only need motivation which helps get one through life complications, but transformation that leads individuals into their manifestation.

  • New Challenges

    The Strengthening of Self

    Today, Trikeria looks to grow and better her brand, through her publications, her online presence, and her sense of tenacity. As an entrepreneur and global citizen, she feels a calling toward helping those around her become better. As a student of the world she strives to constantly learn, grow, and strengthen who she is, so that she can share that with those she helps.
