Who is Trikeria Johnson?

Philanthropist, author, and transformational speaker, Trikeria Johnson was born in Columbia, SC to a single mother who knew from day one, that she was destined to be triumphant. Throughout her time in high school, and at Howard University , wwhile excelling in school, she was involved in a number of extracurriculars, including being the captain of the track team, as well as a leader of her university, as Miss College of Arts and Sciences. In these leadership positions, Trikeria has been able to develop a thorough understanding of what it means to be a leader, and how to grow yourself into positions that may be bigger than yourself. She brings all of that wisdom into her first book "Triumphant Risings", a a 40 day devotional about becoming the best version of yourself. She believes that people not only need motivation which helps get one through life complications, but transformation that leads individuals into their manifestation.
