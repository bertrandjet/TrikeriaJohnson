Triumphant Risings

Triumphant Risings: Affirm Your Rising and Wake with the Day is a 40 day affirmation book to help aid you in setting forth your intentions with the rising of each day. As we are constantly creating and co-creating our realities we must be intentional in doing so. Life and death are in the power of the tongue and by speaking into yourself you are unleashing the limitless power to manifest what you speak into your present reality.
